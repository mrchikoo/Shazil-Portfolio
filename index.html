Deep Learning for Gesture-to-Speech Translation

transformer cnn lstm tensorflow mediapipe

Designed and trained an end-to-end deep-learning pipeline for real-time Indian Sign Language recognition. Combined Transformer, CNN (ResNet) and LSTM architectures, built the full data-processing pipeline with MediaPipe feature extraction, and integrated NLP with text-to-speech for multilingual output.
